About Total Care Reports

Leading the UK in professional medico-legal reporting services with over 15 years of expertise

Our Story

Founded in 2013, Total Care Reports has grown to become one of the UK's most trusted medico-legal reporting services. We started with a simple mission: to provide accurate, impartial, and timely medical reports that help legal professionals make informed decisions.

Over the years, we have built a network of over 150 qualified medical professionals across England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland. Our team includes specialists in various medical fields, ensuring we can provide expert reports for any type of case.

We pride ourselves on maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and integrity, earning the trust of leading law firms, insurance companies, and government agencies across the UK.

Our Mission & Values

Guided by principles that ensure excellence in every report we deliver

Accuracy

We ensure every medical report is meticulously researched, thoroughly documented, and factually accurate. Our medical professionals follow strict protocols to maintain the highest standards of precision.

Impartiality

We maintain complete neutrality in all our reports, providing unbiased medical assessments that serve the interests of justice and truth, regardless of which party has commissioned the report.

Timeliness

We understand the importance of meeting legal deadlines. Our streamlined processes ensure reports are delivered on time without compromising on quality or thoroughness.

Confidentiality

We maintain the strictest confidentiality standards, ensuring all patient information and case details are protected according to medical and legal privacy requirements.
