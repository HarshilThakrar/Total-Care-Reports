Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our medico-legal reporting services

How quickly can you deliver a report?

Our standard turnaround time is 5-7 days for most reports. Urgent reports can be delivered within 24-48 hours for an additional fee. We always discuss timelines with you before starting work.

What information do you need to prepare a report?

We typically need medical records, witness statements, photographs of injuries (if applicable), and any relevant legal documents. We'll provide a detailed checklist when you contact us.

Do you provide expert witness testimony?

Yes, our qualified medical professionals can provide expert witness testimony in court. We have extensive experience in courtroom proceedings and cross-examination.

Are your reports accepted by all UK courts?

Yes, our reports are prepared by GMC-registered medical professionals and comply with all UK legal requirements. They are accepted by courts across England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland.

What are your fees?

Our fees vary depending on the complexity of the case and type of report required. Personal injury reports start from £450, while expert witness testimony starts from £800 per day. We provide detailed quotes before starting work.
